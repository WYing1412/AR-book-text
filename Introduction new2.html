Who I am ?

I am a teacher, I can teach you writing skills, including the use of symbols, paragraphs, and topic sentences through lots of examples.

 

How can I help you ?

Improve your writing. Help you to write a clear, expressive essay, and - most important - under your control. I can help you organize thinking and put down on the page.

How can I improve your life and work ?

 

You will know how to structure your thoughts, choose your words, and bring people along into your ideas with you. If you can write exactly what you mean, you have the edge on those who cannot.
